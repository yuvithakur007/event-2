Technical Departments

The Technical Department meets any critical gaps in technological needs, it identifies and arranges to extend services and material support from original equipment manufacturers. The Technical aspects of our club include: Design and Manufacturing, Avionics, Software Development Cell and Research & Development Department.

Parth Pore
Head : Design & Manufacturing

OTHER MEMBERS  ⇛
This technical Department is responsible for designing the products that are going to be developed in club Anterix. Whether it may be any kind of drones, model aircrafts and rockets, satellites, 3D parts etc. All the mechanical operations associated with the manufacturing and development of the products will all be looked after by this department. The club emphasizes high importance on a design that could be reliably and easily manufactured. Hobbyists that have gained some experience in constructing and flying from plans will often venture into building custom planes from scratch. This involves finding drawings of full-sized aircraft and scaling these down, or even designing the entire airframe from scratch.

Prithviraj Mitra
Head : Electrical/Avionics

OTHER MEMBERS  ⇛
Electrical/Avionics department is responsible for looking after the power system of the Drones, Model Aircrafts , Satellites etc. They will basically work on conversion of one form of energy to another. An aircraft electrical system is a self contained network of components that generate, transmit, distribute, utilize and store electrical energy. Communications, navigation, monitoring, aircraft flight control systems are the domains under this department.

Anuj Negi
Head : SDC

OTHER MEMBERS  ⇛
Software Development Cell provides a platform for coding enthusiasts to develop softwares in the field of Aviation and Space Technology. This department is responsible for all the software requirements that are needed in our product. It will help the students to master their coding skills in various domains such as assembly language, web development, OOP [object oriented programming ] etc. A website is an integral part of online marketing which plays an important role in the digital world. Also, Web Developers will be responsible for the development and maintenance of the Club’s website. A digital presence gives an ideal platform to communicate with investors, sponsors and many others.

Prithviraj Mitra
Head : R&D Cell

Renovation and Innovation related to the product before manufacturing will be looked after by this department. Also a number of analyses will be done before the actual manufacturing of the product. Several groups under this cell will develop inventive ideas for the final deployment. Unconventional proposals can be selected as blogs or will be published in international journals under guidance of faculties irrespective of area of study.
