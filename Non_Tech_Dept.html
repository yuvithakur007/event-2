Non - Technical Departments

The Non-Technical Department maintains the balance of the club and ensures efficient and effective operations. The Non-Technical Aspects of our club include: Documentation & Content Writing, Media, Finance, Logistics & Sponsorship Department.

Pratham Wanmode
Head : Media

OTHER MEMBERS  ⇛
Media are the communication outlets or tools used to store and deliver information currently going on in the club through social media. Media club provides coverage for all types of on and off campus activities and events. It aims to target a large audience which helps in promoting the club. It provides a platform to photographers, videographers,editors and graphic designers as contributors of stories, photos, videos and posters. It also brings forth the students with the opportunity to express their ideas and talent through the media.

Anshika Sinha
Head : Documentation

OTHER MEMBERS  ⇛
The Documentation Department looks after various technical reports required for events or manufacturing of aircrafts, drones and satellites. The department takes care of preparing all the necessary documents which specifies the workflow and previous records of the tasks done by various departments. The documents prepared are detailed research work which will be useful for the technical team. This department also helps technical writers to improve their writing skills.
Currently, we are working on three types of reports:
● Design Report
● Application Report
● Fabrication Report

Allen Singh
Head : Finance

OTHER MEMBERS  ⇛
A finance department is a great way to gain insightful experience into the world of accounting and investment. It helps the team stay organised in the transactions made while dealing and it also maintains transparency within the club. Finance includes purchase of manufacturing materials, registration of events, logistic expenses and other miscellaneous payments. One of the targets of this club is to guide students in pursuit of careers in finance and develop their interpersonal skills.

Pratyush Dubey
Head : Logistics & Sponsorship

OTHER MEMBERS  ⇛
The logistics department looks after obtaining, producing, and distributing the materials required for manufacturing in the right quantities to the final destination. It includes the science of planning, implementing, and managing procedures for the most efficient and effective storage and transportation of manufactured aircrafts, drones and satellites as possible. Sponsorship is one of the most powerful marketing tools . It can also be known as giving your target audience a support but at the same time, sponsorship will be helping you meet your team goals. The aim of the department is to attract investors and sponsors to make the initiative of manufacturing different unmanned aerial vehicles.
