Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is this club about?

    Basically club Antriex deals with the field of aviation and Space technology. Aeromodelling, Drone Manufacturing, model Rocketry, Inter and Intra College Aeromodelling Competitions ,Drone Racing and satellite designing are the domains that we'll be working on.

  • Who all can join this club?

    Students from every domain/stream who are interested nad passionate about the field of aviation and space technology can jon this club.

  • How and when can we join this?

    Within a short period of time we'll issue a google form to join the club . One has to fill that form to be a member of our club. Further interviews will take place for respective departments.

  • Is there any membership fee to join this club?

    No, for now you don't have to pay any membership fee . But once the college starts in offline mode you have to pay the amount of rs.200 as per decided by DSW of VIT Bhopal.

  • If one dosen't have knowledge about aviation field can he/she join this club?

    Yes! you can. It's not necessary that you shoukd have a prior knowledge in the field. You'll be taught and given hands on experience about everything you regulate at Anterix .
