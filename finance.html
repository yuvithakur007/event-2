ABOUT VIT BHOPAL

VIT Bhopal University, located at the Bhopal-Indore highway, an arterial street connecting the two most lovely and cleanest towns of India is a place where information meets motivation. VIT Bhopal University imparts futuristic technical education and inculcate high patterns of discipline through dedicated faculty, who set global standards, making students technologically advanced and ethically strong, who in turn enhance the quality of life of the human race.
